# 🧠 PulsIQ — The Intelligence of Health

> Turning complex health data into clear, confident decisions.

---

## 🌟 Overview

PulsIQ is an AI-powered health intelligence platform designed to simplify medical information and reduce confusion in healthcare.

It transforms scattered health data—like prescriptions, symptoms, and vitals—into easy-to-understand insights, helping users make better health decisions with confidence.

---

## 🤖 Meet Pulsy

Pulsy is the core AI mentor of PulsIQ.

Unlike typical chatbots, Pulsy is designed to be:
- Empathetic 🤝
- Intelligent 🧠
- Easy to understand 💬

It guides users through medical information, explains reports, and reduces anxiety around health issues.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🧾 Prescription Scanner
- Scan handwritten or printed prescriptions
- Get simplified explanations of medicines and usage

### 🩺 Symptom Analyzer
- Enter symptoms and receive AI-based insights
- Understand possible conditions (not a diagnosis)

### 📊 Health Data Tracker
- Track vitals like heart rate, BP, etc.
- Visual insights for better monitoring

### 💡 Smart Health Suggestions
- Personalized recommendations
- Lifestyle and precaution advice

### 🧠 AI Health Mentor (Pulsy)
- Conversational assistant
- Explains medical terms in simple language

## 🎯 Vision
